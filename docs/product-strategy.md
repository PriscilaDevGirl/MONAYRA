# Monayra Product Strategy

## Vision

Create a hiring platform that recognizes potential, protects dignity, and restores access to work for people who are frequently excluded from traditional recruiting flows.

## What makes Monayra different

- No human profile photos
- Social-name-first experience
- Sensitive data hidden until hiring
- Company access controlled by authenticated accounts
- Restart mode for candidates returning to work
- Humanized resume formats with text, audio, and video
- Empathetic feedback as a platform standard

## Main users

### Candidates

- women seeking their first job
- women returning to the workforce
- trans women and travestis facing discrimination
- people with strong practical skills but limited formal experience

### Companies

- inclusive employers
- HR teams trying to shorten time-to-hire
- social impact hiring programs
- diversity and inclusion leaders

## Key metrics

- time to hire
- percentage of candidates receiving feedback
- number of restart-mode candidates hired
- percentage of hiring processes with sensitive data kept protected until final stage
- retention after 90 days

## Monetization ideas

- subscription for company dashboards
- pay-per-vacancy publishing
- premium bias audit reports
- training package for inclusive hiring teams
